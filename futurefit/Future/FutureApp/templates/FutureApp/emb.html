Embedded System

3-months Intensive Program

Embedded Systems Deep dive

Start your Hardware design journey with our hands on course which involves conceptualizing, designing, and creating physical components for electronic devices and systems. Join the 3 months Embedded systems deep dive program to learn electronic circuit schematics capture, design PCBs, and generate gerber files for manufacturing. Beyond the basics, apprentices will leverage our advanced laboratory with Oscilloscopes, multimeters, and assorted development boards. Including how to program firmwares and configure appropriate displays in production ready IoT projects.

Expected Outcomes

By the End of Three Months:

  • Apprentice will be proficient in basic electronics and power electronics.
  • Skilled in using KiCAD for schematic capture, PCB design, and generating files for manufacturing.
  • Knowledgeable in embedded system programming, communication protocols, and display integration.
  • Prepared to design, test, and build their own embedded systems projects with ESP32 or similar microcontrollers.
