3D Design and Printing

1-months Intensive Program

Designing and Animating 3D Casing and PCB Models

Gain a comprehensive understanding of 3D Designing principles, 3D Printing technologies, and practical skills to design and print custom casings for embedded systems project Join this one month intensive 3D design, Animation, and Printing with Blender.

Expected Outcomes

By the end of this 1-month curriculum, apprentices will:

  • Understand the fundamentals of Blender for 3D modeling, including object manipulation, editing, and modifiers.
  • Be able to design and optimize a 3D casing for an electronic project.
  • Gain hands-on experience in preparing models for 3D printing, including slicing and support settings.
  • Learn basic rendering, lighting, and animation techniques to showcase 3D models for electronic circuit PCB models.
